Stanescu Map Projection

A compromise map projection made by Constantin-Ciprian Stanescu on December 25, 2025,
based on a regular octahedron layout similar to Cahill's "Butterfly", and Keyes' "M-shaped" projections, but targeting the simplicity and familiarity of the Mercator projection.
Principal dividing meridian is 0°W, to show almost all the continents and countries uninterrupted. Map ratio is 1:0. Printed & cropped 2D map can be folded into a 3D octahedron Earth.
